The Prova Story

Imagine two idiots sitting in the back of math, twiddling their thumbs, and waiting for the class to end. That was our math class, those were our thumbs, and we both wanted to do something big. We tasked ourselves with searching for products that just aren't that good, and that day, we noticed that the backpacks we were using sucked. Well, less specifically all backpacks suck - if you're a student.

You see, the backpack wasn't designed recently. In fact, the backpack design we all are used to came about in 1984 (the same year the Motorola DynaTAC came out) and it hasn't progressed much since. Everywhere we looked seemed to confirm our theory - nobody really loved thier backpacks. They broke often or they were too expensive, and they were all a bit cumbersome. We set off to fix this problem.

We spent a few days brainstorming differient ways to fabricate a bag and, with the help of our good friend Sam Ho, we came up with the bag featured on this page. We then partnered with Tuff products to get them made and we can't wait to get one issn your hands.

So that's the Prova story... so far. Here's to the future, and to an alternative to shitty bags.

Cheers, Jonathan and Cameron
